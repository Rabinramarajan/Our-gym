Our Gym Offers

Strength

-: The FlexiBell :-

Our innovative, modular FlexiBell replaces multiple sets of dumbbells, by offering an all-in-one dumbbell with adjustable weights. Switch from one weight to another with the simple turn of a dial.

-: The Flex Barbell :-

The Ultimate Barbell, thats fully adjustable. Goes all the way from 0 - 20Kg or 0-38Kg in multiple weight increments (Depending on the model). The Flex Barbell comes with a crocodile tooth concept weight attachment system that makes weight changes quick like lightning.

-: The Flexikettle :-

The innovative FlexiKettle replaces 7 different sets of traditional kettlebells and combines them into 1 smart, easy-to-use FlexiKettle. It comes with an ergonomic handle and a weight selection dial to choose your desired weight level.
